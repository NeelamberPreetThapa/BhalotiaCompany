Our story

Half a century of trust, one decade at a time.

What began as a small home furnishings shop in Chas has grown into Bokaro's go-to name for custom interiors — without ever losing the personal attention of a family business.

1972

The shop opens in Chas

Bhalotia & Company begins as a home furnishing store serving the Bokaro township, focused on bedding and curtains.

1990s

Expanding into furniture

Custom sofas, chairs and headboards are added to the catalogue as demand grows for made-to-order seating.

2000s

Flooring & wall decor introduced

Wooden and PVC flooring, wallpapers and carpets are introduced, turning the store into a full home-decor destination.

Today

Trusted by industry

Now serving residential clients alongside major corporate accounts — Bokaro Steel Plant (SAIL), Electrosteel (ESL) and Dalmia Cement Bokaro.
